Commissioner Setup

Tournament Settings
Teams are generated automatically from the number of joined players.

Example: if 6 people join a 60-golfer contest, the app creates 10 ranking tiers of 6 golfers each, then gives every team 1 golfer from each tier for 10 golfers total.

Contest Controls

Locking the contest prevents new entries and generates evenly balanced teams from the world ranking tiers. Reset removes generated teams and unlocks entries.

Golfer Pool — World Rankings

Top 60 golfers by world ranking are split into dynamic tiers sized to the number of joined players, so every team gets one golfer from each tier.
